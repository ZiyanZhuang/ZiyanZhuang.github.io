About Me

Hello! I am Ziyan Zhuang. My work lies at the intersection of biological sciences and computational design. I am driven by a fundamental curiosity: how can we move beyond empirical methods to design complex systems—be it a novel protein or an adaptive AI—using first principles?

Research Vision

Protein Design

Combining MCMC and PINNs to overcome limitations of traditional molecular dynamics. Integrating statistical mechanics with deep learning constraints.

Evolving Agents

Developing an Agent architecture that mimics biological intelligence, featuring cyclical learning (sleep-wake cycles) and evolutionary selection mechanisms.
